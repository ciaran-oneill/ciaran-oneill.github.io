Welcome to NBA Nerds!

NBA Nerds is the ultimate destination for everything basketball. Born from a passion for the game and a respect for the numbers that define it, NBA Nerds is more than just a website - it's a one-stop shop for fans, analysts and enthusiasts alike. Whether you're tracking your favorite player's career, analyzing team performance, or exploring historical records, our extensive database and intuitive tools put a world of basketball statistics at your fingertips.

Delve into the heart of the game with our comprehensive player statistics, team analytics, and in-depth record compilations. Our personalized features allow you to follow the hourneys of your favorite players, monitor your preferred teams, and even predict future legends through our unique 'Legends' feature.

At NBA Nerds, we believe that the beauty of basketball is not just in the final score, but in the stories the data tells. From thrilling championship victories to awe-inspiring personal achievements, every number has a narrative.

So whether you're a casual fan looking to learn more about the game or a seasoned analyst in search of robust data tools, NBA Nerds is your court-side ticket to the world of basketball. Welcome, and dive into the game like never before!

This Day in NBA History

On this day in 2006, the late great Kobe Bryant had one of the greatest performances of all time, scoring 81 points on 21/33 two-point shots, 7/13 three-poitners, and 18/20 free throws in 41 minutes and 56 seconds. This game remains the highest scoring game in the shot clock era, only trailing Wilt Chamberlain's 100 point performance in 1962.
