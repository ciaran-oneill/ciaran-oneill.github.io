Legacies is a groudnbreaking feature on NBA Nerds that offers a unique perspective on the historical impact and overall career achievements of NBA players. It transcends traditional statistics, providing a hollistic view of player's legacy in the realm of professional basketball. By integrating various accolades and career milestones, Legacies presents a comprehensive score that encapsulates a player's contribution to the game

This dynamic system considers not just the numbers but the weight of each achievement, painting an avid picture of a player's career. From mesemrizing All-Star performances to clutch moments in championship games, Legacies brings together the many facets of a player's journey, offering fans, analysts, and enthusiasts an innovative tool to analyze and debate the greatness of former and current NBA legends.

Legacy Score Equation

Legacy Score = (A = Number of All-Star Appearances) +

(B x Number of MVP Awards) +

(C x Number of Championships) +

(D x Number of Finals MVPs) +

(E x Number of All-Star Game MVPs)

MVP Awards (B): 30 points

Championships (C): 25 points

Finals MVPs (D): 20 points

All-Star Game MVPs (E): 10 points
